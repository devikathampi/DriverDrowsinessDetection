Key Features

Features and Services included in SafeDrive: Driver Drowsiness Detection System

Blinking

Tracks eye blinking movements to trigger a Drowsiness Alert.

Yawning

Tracks yawning movements to trigger a Drowsiness Alert.

Voice Alert

Sends a voice alert when the driver starts becoming drowsy

GPS

Sends a SMS witht the GPS location to the emergency contact number.

Talk-to-Me

Talk-to-me will ask to repeat words & help the driver self-assess his/her alertness.
